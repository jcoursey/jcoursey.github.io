Automatic Image Captioning

Image Captioningclose

Decoded images from the Flickr 8k image dataset using ResNet-152 and automatically produced captions by feeding CNN outputs into an LSTM network. Utilized temperature for varied sampling and experimented with applying dropout at different layers for varied performance.

Katz Back-Off Language Modeling

Language Modelingclose

Trained a model from three corpora and trained a statistical trigram model with discount smoothing and bigram + unigram model interpolation (Katz Back-Off Model) to randomly generate sentences and compute perplexity on various other corpora.

Paper

Business Visit & Category Prediction

Business Predictionclose

Using the Google User-Business dataset, I built classifiers to predict the business category of a given text review and predict whether a user would have visited a given business or not. I feature engineered using Jaccard and cosine similarity, bag-of-words approaches, and IDF-TF to achieve accuracies > 70% on a hidden test set.

Fashion MNIST Classification Analysis

Fashion MNISTclose

Performed exploratory data analysis with PCA and data balance checking on the Fashion-MNIST competition on Kaggle. Evaluated and compared the performances of SVMs with a linear kernel, random forests, multi-layer perceptrons, and CNN's to classify the clothing item displayed in each image.
